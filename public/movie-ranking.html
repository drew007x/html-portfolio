The Best Movies According to Me

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Godfather

A cinematic masterpiece that masterfully explores themes of power, loyalty, and family within the Italian-American Mafia, delivering an iconic narrative that has left a lasting impact on film history.

The Texas Chainsaw Massacre

A classic horror film known for its intense atmosphere, terrifying villain, and groundbreaking approach to the slasher genre, leaving a lasting impact on horror cinema.

The Departed

A tense, masterfully crafted crime thriller directed by Martin Scorsese that explores themes of loyalty, betrayal, and identity.
